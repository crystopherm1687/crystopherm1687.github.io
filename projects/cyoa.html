Invasion of the Skibidi Toilets

Content

Inside this adventure you will see how fate treats you in the face of the Skibidi Toilet apocalyse which can be benefical or harmful. Here you make the choices that you want to decide your future path. Discover all sorts of endings and events that happen within the story to see if you survive or not.

Challenges

Some of the challenges that our group encountered was the formatting of the links because we struggled a bit to make so that when a link was clicked on our project it would go to that page directly with no errors since that was one of the project requirements as well as adding the words themselves since we had to come up with some ideas on the spot to fill in more of our project and seem professional.

Takeaways

Some of the takeaways we can learn from this task is that you should focus a bit on everything so that you you aren't on one thing which could your project progress down but there are different ways to go about it since eventually you will finish it on time. Also preparing information to say beforehand helps with time as you could doze off from the task for some days and not do it until the last day which causes stress.
